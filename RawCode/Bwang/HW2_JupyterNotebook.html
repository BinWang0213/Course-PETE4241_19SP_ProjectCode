(a) Porosity by Athy's equation¶

The vertical stress can be calculated in onshore and offshore as follows:
$${{S}_{v}}={{\bar{\rho }}_{bulk}}g{{z}_{0}}+\int\limits_{{{z}_{0}}}^{z}{{{\rho }_{bulk}}(z)gdz} \quad \text{Onshore} $$ $$ {{S}_{v}}={{\rho }_{w}}g{{z}_{w}}+{{\bar{\rho }}_{bulk}}g({{z}_{0}}-{{z}_{w}})+\int\limits_{{{z}_{0}}-{{z}_{w}}}^{z}{{{\rho }_{bulk}}(z)gdz} \quad \text{Offshore} $$ The pore pressure can be calculated as follows:
$${{p}_{p}}={{\bar{\rho }}_{w}}g{z} $$ The effective vertical stress $\sigma'_v$ can be calculated as follows: $$\sigma'_v=\sigma_v - p_p $$ The porosity can be calculated as follows: $$\phi=\phi_0 e^{-\beta \sigma'_v} $$
